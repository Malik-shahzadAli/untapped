How do challenges benefit businesses?

Boost brand awareness and engagement by hosting a creative challenge.
As users compete they will create specific content and the voters will view specific content relating to the brand hosting. This will increase the reach that usual marketing campaigns get as voting is entering, voting and monitoring is free.
